How To Buy

Step 1: Sign Up

Create your account on our website to get started.

Step 2: Get the email link

Check your email for a verification link and click it to confirm your account.

Step 3: Log in

Return to the website and log in with your new account.

Step 4: Go to Car Stock

Navigate to the Car Stock section to browse available vehicles.

Step 5: Search a car

Use the search filters to find cars that match your preferences. You can filter by: Makers, Models, Body type, Minimum and maximum mileage, Minimum and maximum year, and more.

Step 6: Use Price Calculator

Utilize the TOTAL PRICE CALCULATOR to get a comprehensive cost estimate for your chosen vehicle.

Step 7: Send Message

Once you've found a car you're interested in, click the 'Send Message' button.

Step 8: Select Location

In the product screen, select your country and preferred port.

Step 9: Write Message

Compose your inquiry message in the provided text area.

Step 10: Agree and Send

Check the 'I agree' box and click 'Send Inquiry' to submit your request.
